Memberships

Membership feature provides users with exclusive benefits and privileges that are not available to non-members. Users are typically required to pay a fee to access these benefits, which can be a monthly or yearly subscription.

There are three different membership tiers available:

  • Silver
  • Gold
  • Diamond

Each tier offers different benefits and is designed to meet the needs of users with different budgets and usage requirements.

Silver Membership Tier​

The Silver membership tier is the entry-level tier, offering users access to the following benefits:

  • (x3) daily, this can be up to 10,000 credits in daily
  • 5 reps per day
  • Customizable Background (Profile / ID)
not

This membership tier is ideal for users who have relatively modest needs and are looking for a cost-effective way to access some of the benefits of membership.

Gold Membership Tier​

The Gold membership tier is the mid-level tier, offering users access to the following benefits:

  • (x5) daily, this can be up to 25,000 credits in daily
  • 10 reps per day
  • Customizable Background (Profile / ID)
  • One account transfer per month
not

This membership tier is designed for users who have more substantial needs and require a higher level of functionality than the Silver tier can provide.

Diamond Membership Tier​

The Diamond membership tier is the top-level tier, offering users access to the following benefits:

  • (x10) daily, this can be up to 50,000 credits in daily
  • 30 reps per day
  • Customizable Background (Profile / ID)
  • One account transfer per month
not

This membership tier is ideal for power users who require a high level of functionality and access to exclusive benefits.

Daily on Discord​

The Daily feature on our Discord platform allows users to receive a daily credit amount. The amount of credits awarded varies depending on the user's membership tier, as explained in each membership tier description.

Daily Reps​

The Daily Reps feature enables users to give reputation points to their friends through the use of the /rep command. These points will be displayed on the friend's profile.

Change Background​

The Change Background feature provides users with the option to upload their own unique background image for their profile and ID.

not

It's important to note that supported file types are limited to png, jpeg, and SVG, and that images should not exceed 3MB in size.

tehlike

Inappropriate backgrounds or content, including +18 and NSFW content, are strictly prohibited. Violation of these guidelines may result in the removal of a user's subscription and their ability to subscribe again.

Transfer Per Month​

The Transfer Per Month feature allows users to transfer their old account data, including their level and credits, once per month. To complete this process, users will be asked to provide their old account ID and email address. It's crucial to note that the email address provided must match the email address associated with the old account. Users will receive a 6-digit authentication code via email to complete the transfer process.

not

It's worth noting that the ability to transfer profile data is only available once per month. This restriction ensures that the transfer process is fair and prevents abuse of the feature.

not

It's important to keep in mind that the authentication code is only valid for 15 minutes. If users don't receive a code within that timeframe, they will need to check their email and spam folder or wait for another code.

Memberships Pricing​

To check the prices for our Silver, Gold, and Diamond membership tiers, please visit our website's Memberships Page There, you will be able to compare the benefits of each tier and choose the one that best meets your needs and budget. If you have any further questions or concerns, please feel free to contact our Customer Support team for assistance.
